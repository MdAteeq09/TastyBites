Lachha Paratha

Category: Dinner

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 2–3

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Whole Wheat Flour
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for dough)
  • Extra Ghee for layering and roasting
  • Dry Flour for rolling

Instructions

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, and water to form a soft dough. Rest for 20 mins.
  2. Divide into balls. Roll one into a thin roti. Apply ghee and dust with flour.
  3. Make pleats like a fan, roll into a spiral, and flatten again into a circle.
  4. Heat tawa and roast on both sides with ghee until golden and crispy layers form.
  5. Press gently to open the layers. Serve hot with curry or curd.
