Ras Malai

Category: Dessert

Prep Time: 30 mins | Cook Time: 45 mins

Servings: 4–5

Ingredients

  • 1 liter Full-Fat Milk (for paneer)
  • 2 tbsp Lemon Juice or Vinegar
  • 1 cup Sugar (for syrup)
  • 4 cups Water
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 1/2 liter Full-Fat Milk (for rabri)
  • 3 tbsp Sugar (for rabri)
  • A few Saffron Strands
  • Chopped Pistachios & Almonds (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Boil 1 liter of milk. Add lemon juice to curdle it. Strain and wash to make soft paneer (chhena).
  2. Knead the chhena into a soft dough. Divide and shape into small flattened balls.
  3. Boil water and sugar in a wide pan. Add the chhena balls and cook for 15 minutes until they puff up. Let them cool in the syrup.
  4. In another pan, boil 1/2 liter milk and simmer to thicken into rabri. Add sugar, saffron, and cardamom.
  5. Gently squeeze the chhena balls and transfer them into the prepared rabri. Chill for a few hours.
  6. Garnish with nuts and serve cold.
