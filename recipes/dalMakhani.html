Dal Makhani

Category: Dinner

Prep Time: 15 mins (plus soaking) | Cook Time: 45 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Whole Black Urad Dal (Black Lentils)
  • 2 tbsp Rajma (Kidney Beans)
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Fresh Cream
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required

Instructions

  1. Soak urad dal and rajma overnight. Pressure cook with water and salt until soft.
  2. Heat butter in a pan, add cumin seeds, then onions. Sauté till golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, sauté for a minute, then add tomato puree and spices.
  4. Cook until oil separates, then add cooked dal and rajma with water as needed.
  5. Simmer on low heat for 20–25 mins. Stir occasionally and mash a little for creaminess.
  6. Stir in fresh cream and cook for 5 more mins.
  7. Serve hot with rice or naan, topped with butter or extra cream if desired.
