Paratha

Category: Lunch

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 3–4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Whole wheat flour
  • Water (as needed to knead dough)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Oil or ghee (for dough)
  • Ghee or butter (for roasting)
  • Optional stuffing: Boiled potatoes, paneer, or vegetables

Instructions

  1. Mix wheat flour, salt, oil, and water to make a soft dough. Rest for 15–20 mins.
  2. Divide into equal balls. Roll one ball into a circle using a rolling pin.
  3. Add stuffing if using. Fold and seal edges, then roll again gently.
  4. Heat a tawa/pan and cook paratha on both sides using ghee or butter until golden brown.
  5. Serve hot with curd, pickle, or chutney.
