Gulab Jamun

Category: Dessert

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins

Servings: 12–15 balls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Milk Powder
  • 1/4 cup All-Purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1/4 cup Milk (warm, as needed to form dough)
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Soda
  • Oil or Ghee for frying
  • 1.5 cups Sugar
  • 1.5 cups Water
  • 2–3 Cardamom Pods (crushed)
  • Rose Water (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Gradually add warm milk to make a soft dough. Rest for 10 mins.
  3. Divide into small smooth balls without cracks.
  4. In a pan, boil sugar, water, and cardamom to make syrup. Add rose water if using. Keep warm.
  5. Heat oil/ghee on medium flame. Fry jamuns until golden brown.
  6. Drain and immediately soak them in warm sugar syrup for at least 1 hour.
  7. Serve warm or chilled as desired.
