Honey Garlic Chicken

Category: Gym Lovers

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken Breasts (boneless, cut into cubes)
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 3 Garlic Cloves (minced)
  • 3 tbsp Honey
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce (low sodium)
  • 1 tsp Apple Cider Vinegar or Lemon Juice
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Sesame Seeds (optional, for garnish)
  • Chopped Spring Onions (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Season chicken cubes with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat olive oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
  3. Cook chicken until golden brown on all sides. Remove and keep aside.
  4. In the same pan, add minced garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  5. Add honey, soy sauce, and vinegar. Stir and simmer for 1–2 minutes.
  6. Return chicken to the pan and coat with the sauce.
  7. Cook for another 3–4 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  8. Garnish with sesame seeds and spring onions. Serve hot with brown rice or quinoa.
