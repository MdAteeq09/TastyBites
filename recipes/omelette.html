Omelet

Category: Breakfast

Prep Time: 5 mins | Cook Time: 5 mins

Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 small Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Green chili, chopped (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter
  • Fresh coriander leaves, chopped

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl. Add salt, pepper, chopped onions, green chili, and coriander.
  2. Whisk everything together.
  3. Heat oil or butter in a non-stick pan on medium heat.
  4. Pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread it evenly.
  5. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is golden.
  6. Flip and cook the other side for a minute or until cooked through.
  7. Serve hot with bread or toast!
