Veg Pulao

Category: Lunch

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 25 mins

Servings: 3–4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 2 tbsp Oil or Ghee
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds
  • 1 Bay Leaf
  • 1 Onion (sliced)
  • 1 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1 cup Mixed Vegetables (carrot, beans, peas, potato)
  • 2 cups Water
  • Salt to taste
  • Whole Spices (1 cinnamon stick, 2-3 cloves, 2 green cardamoms)

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice for 20 minutes. Drain it later.
  2. Heat oil/ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and whole spices.
  3. Add sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped vegetables and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  6. Add rice and sauté for another 2 minutes.
  7. Pour 2 cups of water, add salt, mix well and cover.
  8. Cook on low flame until the rice is done and water is absorbed.
  9. Fluff gently and serve hot with raita or curry.
