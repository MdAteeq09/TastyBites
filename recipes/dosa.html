Dosa

Category: Breakfast

Prep Time: 10 mins (plus soaking & fermentation) | Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rice
  • 1/2 cup Urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/4 tsp Fenugreek seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as required
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds separately for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter using water. Mix and let it ferment overnight or 8 hours.
  3. Add salt to the fermented batter and mix well.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan or dosa tawa and pour a ladle of batter in the center.
  5. Spread it in a circular motion to make a thin dosa.
  6. Drizzle some oil or ghee around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  7. Fold and serve hot with chutney and sambar!
