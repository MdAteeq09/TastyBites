Poha

Category: Breakfast

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Poha (flattened rice)
  • 1 Onion, finely chopped
  • 1 Green chili, chopped
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard seeds
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric
  • Salt to taste
  • Curry leaves, Lemon juice, Coriander for garnish

Instructions

  1. Rinse the poha in a sieve under running water. Drain and keep aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chili.
  3. Add onions and sauté until soft. Add turmeric and salt.
  4. Add poha and mix well. Cook for 2-3 mins.
  5. Turn off the heat and add lemon juice. Garnish with coriander.
  6. Serve hot!
