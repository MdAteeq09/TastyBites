Idli

Category: Breakfast

Prep Time: 10 mins (plus fermentation) | Cook Time: 10-12 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Rice
  • 1 cup Urad dal (black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp Fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for greasing

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak rice and urad dal (with fenugreek) separately for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind dal to a smooth batter, then grind rice to a slightly coarse batter.
  3. Mix both batters, add salt, and let it ferment overnight or for 8 hours.
  4. Grease idli moulds and pour the batter into them.
  5. Steam in an idli steamer for 10–12 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let them cool slightly and remove from moulds.
  7. Serve hot with sambar and coconut chutney!
