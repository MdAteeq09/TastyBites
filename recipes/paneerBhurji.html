Paneer Bhurji

Category: Gym Lovers

Prep Time: 10 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 2–3

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (crumbled)
  • 1 tbsp Oil or Butter
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 Tomato (finely chopped)
  • 1 Green Chili (finely chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Red Chili Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam Masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh Coriander (chopped)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili, sauté for a minute.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  4. Now add turmeric, red chili powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add crumbled paneer and mix everything gently. Cook for 2–3 minutes.
  6. Garnish with chopped coriander and serve hot with roti or bread.
