Sheer Kurma

Category: Dessert

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins

Servings: 4–5

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Vermicelli (seviyan)
  • 2 tbsp Ghee
  • 1 liter Full Cream Milk
  • 1/4 cup Sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 6–8 Dates (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp Mixed Nuts (almonds, cashews, pistachios, chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Raisins
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 2 tbsp Condensed Milk (optional, for richness)
  • Rose Water or Kewra Essence (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan. Roast vermicelli until golden brown. Remove and keep aside.
  2. In the same ghee, lightly fry chopped nuts, raisins, and dates. Set aside.
  3. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. Simmer and stir for 10–15 minutes until it thickens slightly.
  4. Add roasted vermicelli and cook until soft (5–7 mins).
  5. Add sugar, cardamom powder, and condensed milk. Mix well.
  6. Stir in the fried dry fruits and dates.
  7. Simmer for another 5 minutes. Add rose water or kewra essence if using.
  8. Serve warm or chilled, as preferred.
