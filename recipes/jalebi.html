Jalebi

Category: Dessert

Prep Time: 15 mins | Fermentation: 6–8 hrs | Cook Time: 20 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour (Maida)
  • 2 tbsp Cornflour
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/2 cup Yogurt (curd)
  • Water as needed (to make batter)
  • 1 pinch Yellow/Orange Food Color (optional)
  • Oil or Ghee (for frying)

For Sugar Syrup:

  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 1/2 cup Water
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder
  • 1/2 tsp Lemon Juice
  • A few strands of Saffron (optional)

Instructions

  1. Mix maida, cornflour, baking powder, yogurt, and water to make a smooth, flowing batter. Add food color if using.
  2. Cover and ferment the batter in a warm place for 6–8 hours.
  3. Meanwhile, prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water until sticky (1-string consistency). Add lemon juice, cardamom powder, and saffron. Keep warm.
  4. Heat oil/ghee in a wide pan. Fill the batter into a squeeze bottle or piping bag.
  5. Squeeze spiral shapes into hot oil. Fry on medium heat until golden and crisp.
  6. Immediately dip the fried jalebis into warm sugar syrup for a few seconds.
  7. Remove and serve hot. Enjoy with rabri for extra indulgence!
