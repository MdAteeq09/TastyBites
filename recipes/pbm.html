Paneer Butter Masala

Category: Lunch

Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 25 mins

Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Oil
  • 1 cup Tomato puree
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/4 cup Fresh cream
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric
  • 1 tsp Coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Kasuri methi (dried fenugreek) – 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Add onions and sauté until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1–2 mins.
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  4. Add turmeric, chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Add cream, garam masala, and crushed kasuri methi. Stir gently.
  6. Add paneer cubes and cook for 5 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with naan, roti, or rice!
