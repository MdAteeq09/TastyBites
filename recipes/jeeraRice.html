Jeera Rice

Category: Lunch

Prep Time: 20 mins | Cook Time: 15 mins

Servings: 3–4

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati Rice
  • 2 cups Water
  • 1 tbsp Ghee or Oil
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds (Jeera)
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: Chopped Coriander

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain the water.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  3. Add the soaked rice and sauté gently for 2 minutes.
  4. Add 2 cups of water and salt. Mix well.
  5. Cover and cook on low flame until rice is fully cooked and water is absorbed.
  6. Fluff with fork and garnish with coriander.
