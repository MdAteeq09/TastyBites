Fruit Smoothie

Category: Gym Lovers / Breakfast / Snack

Prep Time: 5 mins | Cook Time: —

Servings: 1–2

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Strawberries
  • 1/2 cup Mango chunks
  • 1/2 Banana
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt or Curd
  • 1/2 cup Orange Juice or Water
  • 1 tsp Honey or Maple Syrup (optional)
  • Ice Cubes (as needed)

Instructions

  1. Add all the fruits, yogurt, and juice to a blender.
  2. Add ice cubes and honey if using.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and serve immediately chilled.
